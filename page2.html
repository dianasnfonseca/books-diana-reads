My Bookshelf

English Books

The Amazons: Lives and Legends of Warrior Women across the Ancient World

Genre: History   Author: Adrienne Mayor    Goodreads

I read most of the chapters of this book. A mix of hilarious and brutal - fascinating read that balances myth with historical insight.

Invisible Women: Data Bias in a World Designed for Men

Genre: Non-fiction   Author: Caroline Criado Pérez    Goodreads

Why women are 50% more likely to be misdiagnosed after a heart attack? Why women are 17% more likely to die in a car crash? Very insightful book.

Over the Edge of the World: Magellan's Terrifying Circumnavigation of the Globe

Genre: History   Author: Laurence Bergreen    Goodreads

* Spoiler * – it didn’t end well for Magellan when he attempted to colonize the Philippines.

The Culture Map: Breaking Through the Invisible Boundaries of Global Business

Genre: Business   Author: Erin Meyer    Goodreads

This genre is not my favourite. I got it as it was recommended by my previous manager. I actually just looked for every page where Portugal was referenced and had fun reading what people thought about Portuguese behaviour.

The Sayings of the Vikings

Genre: Poetry   Author: Björn Jónasson    Goodreads

I got this book on my trip to Iceland! Below you can see a video of one of the coolest waterfall I have ever seen - Skógafoss.


To Kill a Mockingbird

Genre: Fiction   Author: Harper Lee    Goodreads

Classic: Timeless story of childhood, justice, and moral growth. It tackles heavy issues like racism and empathy with warmth and wisdom.

The Fire in the Equations

Genre: Science   Author: Kitty Ferguson    Goodreads

Still on going. Delves into the intersection of science and religion, exploring the mysteries of the universe. It challenges readers to ponder profound questions about existence, faith, and the nature of reality.

Portuguese Books

A Teoria de Tudo: A Origem e o Destino do Universo

Genre: Science   Author: Stephen Hawking    Goodreads

I think it offers offers a profound exploration of the universe's origins and future, blending scientific rigor with accessible insights

Os Incêndios Florestais Em Portugal

Genre: Science   Author: António Bento-Gonçalves    Goodreads

I found this book in the Francisco Manuel dos Santos Foundation's collection, and it delves into Portuguese wildfires, particularly the devastating 2017 events. It hasn't been translated into English, but if you're interested, you can watch DiCaprio's documentary on the topic here.

O Guardador de Rebanhos

Genre: Poetry   Author: Alberto Caeiro, Fernando Pessoa    Goodreads

This book is so heartwarming! I love reading it whenever I feel homesick. It’s even been translated into Esperanto! Below, you can listen to me reading one of the poems in English.
